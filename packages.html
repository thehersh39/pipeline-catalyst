Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't you show pricing on the website?

Every business is unique, with different team sizes, sales cycles, and sequence needs. We provide custom quotes based on your specific requirements to ensure you only pay for what you need. Our pricing is transparent and competitive—we just need to understand your needs first.

What's included in the discovery session?

We spend 90 minutes understanding your buyers, your solution's unique value, your sales process, tech stack, team structure, and what messaging has worked in the past. This isn't a surface-level chat—it's a deep dive that ensures your sequences sound like they came from your best rep.

How are you different from hiring a copywriter?

Most copywriters don't understand SaaS sales cycles, multi-channel orchestration, or what actually gets prospects to respond. We bring a decade of top-performing SaaS sales experience, not just writing skills. Your sequences are built by someone who's actually booked thousands of meetings.

Can we start with Foundation and upgrade later?

Absolutely! Many clients start with Foundation to prove the ROI, then expand to additional sequences as they grow. We can add new sequences, personas, or team members whenever you're ready.

Do you guarantee results?

While we can't guarantee specific meeting numbers (too many variables), we do guarantee sequences built on proven frameworks that top performers use. Most clients see significant improvements within 30-60 days, typically moving from 3-4 meetings to 10-12 meetings per 1,000 emails.
