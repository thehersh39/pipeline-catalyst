How Sales Simplified Works

From understanding your unique business to delivering ready-to-send sequences in 10 days

Our 4-Step Process

We don't hand you generic templates. We build custom sequences specifically for your business.

1

Discovery & Deep Dive

We start with a comprehensive 90-minute discovery session where we learn everything about your business, not just surface-level information.

  • Your specific buyer personas and their pain points
  • What makes your solution unique in the market
  • Your sales team structure and current process
  • Your tech stack and how sequences will integrate
  • Past messaging that has worked (and what hasn't)
🎯
2

Custom Sequence Creation

Using insights from our discovery, we craft sequences that sound like they came from your best rep on their best day - because they're built on real understanding of your business.

  • Multi-channel approach (email, phone, LinkedIn)
  • Your unique value props woven throughout
  • Industry-specific language and references
  • Compelling subject lines tested for your market
  • Call scripts that feel natural to your team
✍️
3

Review & Refinement

Your sequences go through our proven quality process, then we collaborate with you to ensure every message aligns with your brand voice and goals.

  • Internal review against top performer benchmarks
  • Your team reviews and provides feedback
  • We refine until every sequence is perfect
  • Final approval from your stakeholders
  • Ready-to-deploy formatting for your systems
🔄
4

Implementation & Training

We don't just hand over documents and disappear. We ensure your team knows exactly how to use these sequences for maximum impact.

  • Sequences formatted for your specific tools
  • Team training on when and how to use each sequence
  • Best practices guide for personalization
  • A/B testing recommendations
  • 30-day follow-up to track initial results
🚀
